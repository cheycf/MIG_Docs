Dataset¶

The folder resources.MIGG contains dataset used by MIGG.

  • Sumatra

    • Liu-2020-JGR-data: seismic traveltime data in Liu et al. (2020). See the latest version in https://doi.org/10.21979/N9/EBQHFG.

    • Liu-2019-JGR-data: seismic traveltime data in Liu et al. (2019). See the latest version in https://doi.org/10.21979/N9/JANKP9.

  • 2020-USTC-seismological-algorithms: a seismological algorithm training workshop in 2020 at USTC. Also see linkresearcher and bilibili for videos, codes, and ppts.

  • 2018-USTC-seismological-algorithms: a seismological algorithm training workshop in 2018 at USTC

Notes¶

The folder notes.MIGG contains some notes in MIGG.

  • NTU-study-notes-HuiLi: NTU study summary by Hui Li
